Brooklyn Tech's Majors

The Majors of Brooklyn Tech

Brooklyn Tech has a total of 18 majors. During your sophomore year you'll see presentations regarding each major before you rank your major. Brooklyn Tech has you rank all 18 majors in a list before giving you your major based on your rank as well as your gpa in certain academic courses. At the end of your sophomore year The most popular majors in Brooklyn Tech are, Law & Society or LAS, Finance, and lastly Software Engineering. Brooklyn Tech's LIU Advanced Health Professions, and LIU PharmD are majors that will allow you to enter the neighboring college, Long Island University as a sophomore instead of a freshman.

Extended Opportunities

  • Consolidated Edison Summer Internship Program
    Founded by Dr. M. Mandery, a Brooklyn Tech alumni and the CEO of the Brooklyn Tech Alumni Foundation, this program hires approximately 15 interns to be assigned a summer project completed under the guidance of a Con Edision Mentor.
    Requirements:
    - Above an 85 average
    - Must be a junior in Computer Science, Applied Physics, Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics and Robotics, or Applied Mathematics
    - Must be able to work 9 weeks of summer, June 29th - August 28th and must possess valid working papers

Aerospace Engineering

Aerospace Engineering is all encompassing of everything with aircrafts, aviation, and even space. You'll not only be designing and creating aircrafts, but you'll also learn to fly.

Junior Courses

  • AP Physics 1 (Double-Period)
  • PLTW Principles of Engineering
    - PLTW stands for Project Lead The Way and can lead to a college credit through the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT)

Senior Courses

  • Flight School (FAA Examination)
    - You'll be takeing the FAA Private Pilot's Knowledge Test. - The course ends with a simulated cross-country flight

Architecture

The Architecture Major is quite what it sounds like, where you learn about the planning, designing and constructing structures.

Junior Courses

  • Architectural Drawing
  • PLTW Civil Engineering & Architecture
    - Can lead to a college credit through Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT)
    - You use Rivet, a 3D design program from Autodesk

Senior Courses

  • Architectual Design/ Modeling
  • AutoCad with Certiport Certification
    - Students will learn how to use AutoCAD and take the AutoCAD certification exam.

Biological Science

Biological Science has you learn about the life sciences such as genetics, and biology. You;ll also have the chance to dissect organisms.

Junior Courses

  • AP Biology (Double-Period)

Senior Courses

  • Genetics (One Semester | Double-Period)
  • Human Anatomy (One Semester | Double-Period)
  • AP Psychology
    - AP Psych is a very popular elective, another reason why Bio Sci is a popular major.

Chemistry

The Chemistry Major doesn't hide anything in its name. It's Chemistry. Chemistry majors take most of the courses required in the first two years of college chemistry. They also have opportunities for intermships and competitions.

Junior Courses

  • AP Chemistry (Double-Period)

Senior Courses

  • Organic Chemistry (One Semester | Double-Period)
  • Quantitative Analysis (One Semester | Double-Period)
  • Intro to Chemical Engineering (One Semester)
  • Moden Chemical Instrumental Analysis (MICA) (One Semester)

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering focuses on the maintenance of structures whether they're man-made or environmental, from buildings to dams, to geology.
There are also many extended learning opportunities such as the American Society of Civil Engineers(ASCE), a student-run organization committed to provide information and resources to the Civil Engineering education. Work-based learning opportunities are also offered such as job shadowing and summer internships like the NYC School Construction Authority (SCA) and the NYC department of Design and Construction (DDC).

Junior Courses

  • Engineering Stactics
  • PLTW Civil Engineering & Architecture
    - Can lead to a college credit through Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT)
    - You use Rivet, a 3D design program from Autodesk
  • AP Physics 1 Optional

Senior Courses

  • American Concreate Institute Certification
  • Land Surveying Certification
  • AutoCad with Certiport Certification
    - Students will learn how to use AutoCAD, and will be introduced to SoftDesk-Civil, another program similar to AutoCAD but specifically for Civil Engineering problems.
    - Students will take the AutoCAD certification exam.
    - Students will have their own professional portfolio of their work for college.

Electrical Engineering

Electircal Engineering is one of the smallest majors in Brooklyn Tech. Electrical Engineering focuses on the design of electronic circuits as well as digital electronics. Electrical Engineering is more mathematics and scientifically based.

Junior Courses

  • AP Physics 1 (Double-Period)
  • PTLW Digital Electronics
    - Students can earn 3 transcripted, college credits from Rochester Institute of Technology(RIT) after completing the course and passing the PLTW Exam at the end of the course

Senior Courses

  • Digital Systems Design
    - This course is credited by the Stony Brook ACE Program
  • AP Physics II (Double-Period)
  • PLTW Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM)
    - Can lead to a college credit through Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT)

Environmental Science

The Environmental Science major gives you access to the equipment in the Con Edison Environmental lab while studying things like ecology, humanity's impact on the environment, agriculture, natural resources, climate change, so on so forth. The Environmental Science major is a relatively popular major partly because its has school trips.

Junior Courses

  • AP Environmental Science (Double-Period)
    - This is the class that takes students on field trips

Senior Courses

  • AP Human Geography
  • PLTW Environmental Sustainability (Double-Period)

Finance

The Finance Major is another popular major. You'll learn about financial services as well as the fundamentals of how businesses are run.

Junior Courses

  • Principles of Accounting
  • AP Macro-Economics
  • AP Micro-Economics

Senior Courses

  • AP Statistics
  • Finance & Investments
  • Introduction to Business Law

Industrial Design

The Industrial Design major combines art with the engineering design processes. The major challenges students to create product solutions and design them using tools like Autodesk Inventor

Junior Courses

  • Fundamentals of Product Design
  • Methods of Fabrication
    - Students will be creating several projects solo, or in a team context using 3D modeling software and machinery
    - Students will be using Autodesk Inventor, Basic hand tools and heavy machinery, and Rapid Prototyping

Senior Courses

  • Advanced Modeling
  • Inventor Certification
    - Students can become an Autodesk Certified User and/or an Autodesk Certified Professional
  • Adobe Visual Design
    - Students can become an Adobe Certified Professional, Photoshop, and or Illustrator

Law and Society

The Law and Society or the LAS Major is another very popular major that aims to expose students to law, both on how they can pursue a career in law, and how law can shape society, focusing on social justice and equity.

Junior Courses

  • Criminal Law
  • Constitutional Law

Senior Courses

  • Criminal Procedure (Fall)
  • Civil Law (Spring)
  • Forensic Criminology
  • AP United States Government & Politics

LIU Advanced Health Professsions

The LIU Advanced Health Professions Major is one of two majors that can allow you to enter Long Island University or LIU nearby Brooklyn Tech as a sophomore if you've completed 30 or more credits towards their degree. While you aren't forced to attend LIU for college, attending LIU can guarantee that your credits will be transfered

Junior Courses

  • AP Biology (Double-Period)
  • AP English Language and Composition (Co-Requisite)
    - Since this course is Co-Requisite, if you fail, you'll have to retake it during your senior year

Senior Courses

  • AP Chemistry (Double-Period)
  • AP Psychology
  • AP Macro-Economics (Co-Requisite)
    - Since this course is Co-Requisite, if you fail, you'll have to retake it

LIU PharmD

The LIU PharmD Major is one of two majors that can allow you to enter Long Island University or LIU nearby Brooklyn Tech as a sophomore if you've completed 30 or more credits towards their degree. While you aren't forced to attend LIU for college, attending LIU can guarantee that your credits will be transfered

Junior Courses

  • AP Chemistry
    - You should aim for an AP Exam grade of 3+
  • AP English Language and Composition
    - You should aim for an AP Exam grade of 3+
  • Algebra 2 or Pre-Calculus (though Pre-Calc is preferred)

Senior Courses

  • AP Macro-Economics
    - You should aim for an AP Exam grade of 3+
  • Pre-Calculus or Calculus AB or Calculus BC (though Calculus BC is preferred)
    - You should aim for an AP Exam grade of 3+ in the AP Calc BC Exam
  • Pharmacy Organic Chemistry and Seminar
  • AP Biology (Double-Period)
    - You should aim for an AP Exam grade of 3+
  • Afterwards, you need to take Psychology and a core studies course over the summer and you'll complete the P1 year!

Applied Mathematics

Otherwise known as the Math Major, the Math major focuses mostly on mathematical methods that are used in science engineering, business, and the industry.

Junior Courses

  • Math Research with Computer Programming
  • Graph Theory (One Semester)
  • Number Theory (One Semeser)

Senior Courses

  • AP Calculus BC or Multivariable Calculus
  • AP Statistics
  • Mathematical Computing
  • AP Macro-Economics

Mechatonics and Robotics

The Mechatronics and Robotics major was originally just Mechatronics for Mechanics and electronics but since then it's been broadened to also include Robotics. The Mechatronics and Robotics Major teaches students through hands-on activities. They will learn the programming language C and will learn to use VEX hardware components

Junior Courses

  • AP Physics I (Double-Period)
  • Robotics Engineering I and II
  • PLTW Digital Electronics

Senior Courses

  • Mechatronics with LabVIEW
    - Students can obtain the LabVIEW Associate Developer Certification, the first step in the three-parter od the LabVIEW certification process
  • PLTW Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM) (Double-Period)

Media

The Media major also offers a bunch of certification exams offered by Adobe and will be assisted by teachers to create an art profilio as part of the college process

Junior Courses

Senior Courses

Physics

The Physics major is one of the smaller majors but it is the most fundamental science as it seeks to explain the most basic and essential level of what the universe is made of.

Junior Courses

Senior Courses

Social Science Research

Or more often known as just the Social Science Major, it focuses on the synergy of humanies and the sciences, watching human behavior through a scientific lens.

Junior Courses

Senior Courses

Junior Courses

Senior Courses
