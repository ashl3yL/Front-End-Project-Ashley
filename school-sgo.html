The Brooklyn Tech SGO Store

The SGO Store

The SGO Store is run by the Student Government, which is why its called SGO, for Student Government Organization. The store is one of the ways the Student Government earns money. In the building, the SGO store usually opens from 4th period to 9th period and is located in the center area of the cafeteria on the 7th floor. Although there is an online website for puchasing the school merch here, I highly reccommend going to the actual room because not only can you actually see what you're buying, but if you're a freshman and need to buy gym gear, the SGO store is likely to have a value package that the online website won't have.
